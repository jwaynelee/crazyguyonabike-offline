This is not an Expedition

It's not even a world tour. (We're cycling from UK to Australia, via the 'Stans).
By Oanh Tran and Nic Geard
#420 - posted Sunday December 5, 2010 by Neil Gunton

"We are two Australians, who have been living in the UK for the past 3 and a half years. It's time we left this green and pleasant land to return to our sunburnt country. If we don't leave of our own accord, we'll be kicked out. We're both rather rule abiding (I'm a lawyer; Nic's an academic; we can't help it), so we'll depart before our visas expire. The plan is to cycle home. As we leave in October, we'll be making a mad dash south to ensure that hypothermia, along with all the other dangers our respective families and friends have envisaged for us, does not kill us. We will over-winter in Morocco and then head back up to the warmer Mediterranean countries in 2011, island hopping our way to Turkey (via Sardinia, Sicily and the Greek Islands). After Turkey, we hope to travel through Iran and Central Asia, politics, wars and visas permitting. We then plan to wend our way through China and SE Asia before, most likely, finding an aeroplane to cross the oceans to Australia-land..."
